The Barangay

Nestled in the heart of Pasig, Barangay Sta. Lucia is a vibrant and welcoming community.

Experience the perfect blend of tradition and modernity as you explore our bustling streets,

lush parks, and engaging local culture. We invite you to be part of our close-knit family,

where unity and camaraderie flourish. Discover the warmth and hospitality of our residents

as you create lasting memories in our beautiful barangay. Welcome home!

MISSION

To be a representation of good governance that gives/improves quality of life, balanced opportunity and create a culpable community to the present and future generations.

VISION

To provide excellent quality of service for the people and create a peaceful community to achieve a positive social change within. To be an instrument of certain social, economic and environmental climb for the people of Barangay Santa Lucia.

GOALS

(Mithiin)

Isang komunidad na may kahandaan sa mga darating na sakuna at kalamidad upang maging matatag at magkaroon ng sapat na kaalaman sa kung ano ang gagawin tuwing may sakuna

OBJECTIVES

(Layunin)

Matatag na komunidad na may wastong kaalaman palaging handa, at matatag na kayang sumugpo sa kahit anong uri ng disaster.
